# Disclaimer

Quick TOTP is an independently maintained fork of the original
[TOTP](https://github.com/dkosmari/gnome-shell-extension-totp) GNOME Shell
extension created by Daniel Kosmari.

## Relationship to the original project

- The original project is acknowledged as the foundation of this work. Its
  source code, design, and documentation made Quick TOTP possible, and its
  history and authorship are preserved in full.
- Quick TOTP is a separate, independently maintained project. It carries its
  own extension identity (UUID, settings schema, and translation domain) so it
  can be installed and published on its own.

## Where development happens

- Future development of Quick TOTP takes place in this repository.
- Issues and feature requests for Quick TOTP should be reported here, in this
  repository's issue tracker.
- The original project continues to belong to its original author; please
  direct questions about the upstream extension to the upstream project.
